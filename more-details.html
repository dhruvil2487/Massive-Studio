Game Mathematician

Job Category: Massive Studio

Job Type: Full Time

Job Location: Portugal and UK

Purpose of the role

  • ● An exciting opportunity exists to join an exciting well funded start-up that will focus on developing slot games on a global scale.
  • ● You will be playing a key role in developing math models for our exciting casino slot games, contributing to the inception and growth of our content portfolio.
  • ● If you enjoy calculating, designing and implementing mathematical concepts, which is the core of our games and will be a critical factor for success in our markets we want to hear from you.
  • ● We are looking for a motivated Mathematician to join a collaborative, stimulating, creative, vibrant and friendly working environment to be based in either the UK or Portugal.

Job Description
Role and Responsibilities

  • ● Experience in creating game math for slots
  • ● Game Mechanic design and knowledge of why mechanics work and can be balanced
  • ● Working with dev teams to enable the fine points of how a game works to be communicated
  • ● Must have knowledge of what players want from a game, industry trends to ensure games keep evolving and lead the way in math balancing
  • ● Uses and provides stats in a defined way to ensure games can be compared to each other
  • ● Discuss with senior management what a game’s aims are, how it should play, what players should it appeal to, etc.
  • ● Be a part of a team making all games as good as possible, providing feedback to other games teams/mathematicians.

Work experience and skills
Essential experience

  • ● A History of designing and delivering game math PAR sheets
  • ● Defining simulators or other methods to enable a game to be balanced
  • ● Discussing the fine points of a game balance and the methods used, and those which could be used
  • ● with other members of the team and taking instructions about aims to deliver balanced game math.

Desirable qualifications

  • ● Game Design/Mechanic design

Personal qualities and behavioral traits
Essential qualities or behaviors:

  • ● Team Player, good communicator, reliable. Able to discuss and define next steps, and overall aims, in a project.
  • ● A good time manager, delivering to agreed timescales

Desirable qualities or behaviors

  • ● Attention to fine detail.

Relationships
Details of relationships relevant to this role

  • ● Management
  • ● Development
  • ● Delivery
